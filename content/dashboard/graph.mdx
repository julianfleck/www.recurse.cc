# Knowledge Graph

Explore the interconnected web of your documents and knowledge. The graph view shows how different concepts, documents, and ideas are related within your RAGE knowledge base.

## Features

- **Interactive Visualization**: Click and drag nodes to explore relationships
- **Search Integration**: Find specific nodes and their connections
- **Multiple Layouts**: Switch between different graph layouts
- **Node Details**: Click on nodes to see detailed information

## Getting Started

*Graph visualization will be integrated here once the dashboard components are migrated.*

## Navigation Tips

- **Zoom**: Use mouse wheel to zoom in/out
- **Pan**: Click and drag the background to move around
- **Node Selection**: Click on nodes to see details
- **Search**: Use the search bar to find specific content
