# Dashboard Overview

Welcome to your RAGE knowledge management dashboard. This is where you'll manage your documents, explore your knowledge graph, and monitor your usage.

## Quick Actions

- **Upload Documents**: Add new content to your knowledge base
- **Explore Graph**: Visualize relationships in your knowledge
- **View Usage**: Monitor your API usage and limits
